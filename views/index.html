The Future of Bitcoin Transactions

Bitswipe is a digital currency payment processing company that specializes in the
processing, integrating and exchanging of digital currencies for merchants and ATM providers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a secure atmosphere for customers to store their digital currencies
as well as providing merchants and ATM related services to accept digital currencies as a form of payment for goods and services.
